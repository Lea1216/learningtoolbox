SOS Mentimeter

In a collorative platform, ask questions about the topic to evaluate the mood of the class. Use the activity to break the ice, create class atmosphere and teacher/student points of contact, and to see if the concepts are being grasped. Ask topic questions to see if the class is being monotonous, challenging, interesting... Depending on the answers adjust the lesson plan - use a video, tell a story, ask questions, divide the class into small groups and create discussion. The results are for example word clouds or bar graphs which allows a discussion phase after the results presentation.

Facilitation Notes

Answers are anonymous and therefore it is the biggest challenge to get everyone to participate. It can get difficult to phrase the questions/challenges in a few words, without making the sentences/requests too "childish". The free version of the Mentimeter app has limitations on the number of words and options.
